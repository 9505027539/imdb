hello is a 2017 Indian Telugu romantic action thriller film, produced by Nagarjuna Akkineni under his Annapurna Studios banner and written & directed by Vikram K Kumar. The film stars Akhil Akkineni and Kalyani Priyadarshan in the lead roles. The music is composed by Anup Rubens. The film released on 22 December 2017. Sreenu is an orphaned street urchin who has a friend named Junnu, whose family has to transfer many times because of her father's job. The day before she left, Junnu gave her phone number to Sreenu written on a hundred rupee note, but that note was stolen by another street boy. Whilst chasing him, he was hit by a car driven by Sarojini (Ramya Krishnan) and her husband Prakash (Jagapathi Babu). They later adopted him.
